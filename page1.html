Placement of Fire Extinguishers

These are some examples on where to place your fire extinguishers.

  1. Near an exit
  2. Visible location
  3. No more than 23m away from a Class A hazard (Ordinary combustibles)
  4. No more than 15m away from a Class B hazard (Flammable liquids)
  5. Away from heat sources
